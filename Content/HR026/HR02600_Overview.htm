Overview

Accrual Plans in COINS OA Human Resources now have a Maximum Accrual Amount for a year. This yearly maximum accrual is determined by a start and end date time period; the start and end dates are created by users and can correspond to certain companies and/or payroll frequencies. The Maximum Accrual Amount field is available on Accrual Plan Rate records and is only used for Rate Per Hour Plans.

The information provided in this document concerning Yearly Maximum Accrual Amounts is relevant to the US, Canadian, and Australian regions.
