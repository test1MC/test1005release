Statements

If the new choices functionality is in use, the types of choices to be included on the statement are driven by the new HS parameter CHOSTATE.

On the Items tab, the Type column shows one of the following values instead of ‘Extra’ and ‘Option’:

  • Standard Specification
  • Free Choice
  • Chargeable Choice
  • Bespoke Choice

In addition, new columns show the area and item to which the choice relates.
