Cancelling a Plot Sale

If the sale of a plot is cancelled, record this in Sales Workbench. 

To record a cancellation:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "cancel sale".
  3. Enter the amount of any refunded.
  4. On the Reason tab, the reason or reasons for the sale being cancelled.
  5. Click .
