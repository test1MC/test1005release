CO/EXPR - Expenses To Payroll Employee

The optional fields in Payroll that are updated when the Expenses record changes.

NOTE

In Human Resources, the are three fields for forename, middle name and ; in Payroll there are two fields for forenames and , and in Expenses there is a single field for (which is sometimes used to hold the full name). If you choose to update or forenames in Payroll from Expenses, COINS separates the names into the appropriate fields according to where the spaces are. So if the field in Expenses was changed to Peter Timothy John Smith, the fields in Payroll would be updated as follows: Forename - Peter Timothy John, - Smith.
