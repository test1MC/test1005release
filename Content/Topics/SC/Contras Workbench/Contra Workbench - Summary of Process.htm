Contra Workbench - Summary of Process

New posted cost transactions that match the contra filter criteria are created as unallocated costs and appear on a new Contra Workbench. You can view all unallocated costs for a specified .

You can match unallocated costs with each other using an action as long as the balance of the transactions add up to zero.

Unallocated costs that cannot be matched can be linked to a contra record so they can later be recovered. Contra records allow details of the recovery, and an administration charge, to be entered.

You then create a invoice for recovery of the costs.

When recovery transactions are posted, they will match the original cost transaction to one or more costing distribution records and mark the transactions as Matched.

invoices for contras will be recovered on Subcontract and payment runs by automatic or manual selection for payment of these transactions during the payment process.
