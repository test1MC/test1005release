Overview

Previously, when the PRHR Company parameter was used (to ensure employee record management between HR and PR modules), the HR Personnel was disabled and could not be entered since it is synced with the PR Employee . For example, if an employee handed in their notice, the HR user was unable to enter the last day of service on the screen.

For the UK Region, this occurred because the Employee is tied to PR processes to ; consequently, the in HR was only updated when the PR status eventually reached number ‘2’, which was after the employee had left. This process resulted in the following issues:

  • Caused the management of large scale redundancies or normal processes to be difficult.
  • Forced users to create fields or use the HR ‘status’ flag to denote a .
  • Produced inaccurate HR reports since employees would often have left but still remained current until Payroll processing was completed.

In an effort to alleviate these issues, Human Resources has been updated to allow the entry of the from the Personnel screen, even if the CO/PRHR parameter has been set, as long as the new parameter HR/HRPRLEAV is set to WORKBENCH.
